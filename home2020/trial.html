Entrepreneurship Development Cell, VIIT

WHAT MAKES US EDC

Entrepreneurship Development Cell(EDC VIIT) was established to nurture entrepreneurial skills and increase the managerial competencies of the aspiring engineers, to create technocrat entrepreneurs. With the goal indenting to inculcate the enterprising spirit, the cell hosts various events and activities throughout the year. Everyone has ideas, but not everyone has the courage to act and execute them. We as Entrepreneurship Development Cell work towards elevating/uplifting the young minds to stimulate, support and sustain their ventures.


OUR INITIATIVES

Vishwapreneur

Vishwapreneur

Vishwapreneur is a National Level Entrepreneurial Summit showcasing entrepreneurs, creators and innovative minds of our century who had the courage to think out of the box. Vishwapreneur is a platform for budding entrepreneurs to seek motivation & guidance through experiences of successful business tycoon.

Corpstrata

Corpstrata

Corpstrata’ comes from Corporate Strategies. In order to introduce the entrepreneurial concepts in an easy and playful manner, Corpstrata comes into picture. From campaigning to documenting and from getting sponsors to learning how to finance that money, Corpstrata exposes the first-year students to everything.

Student Facillation Center

Student Facillation Center

As aptly named, Student Facilitation Centre’s first and foremost aim is to facilitate the students. The Student Facilitation Centre provides the students with all the necessary items required for their first year of engineering, in the form of a basic kit. Ever since its inception, it has been known to be highly convenient. .

Genesis

Genesis

Genesis is organised by the Cell to provide members with a stage to showcase their entrepreneurial pursuits. Year after year, judges come to share their insight and experience with the teams to help them become market ready. In this competition, Each and every team goes through the different phases and are challenged to conceive of a new service or product offering thus prepare heavily to establish the right base for their startups.

E-Cell Meet

E-Cell Meet

The Cell is keen in introducing members to a wide spectrum of possibilities and guiding them in their passion of becoming a true entrepreneur, knowing that E-Cells in Pune are doing a tremendous job of promoting entrepreneurship we came up with a new initiative, The Startup Meet which was held on 8th of January. It is a platform where E-Cells from all over Pune come to interact with each other and build a network of likeminded people.

STARTUPS INCUBATED

Elecer Services Pvt. Ltd

Revolutionizing the way we commute,Elecer has the vision to create a cycling ecosystem and promote a healthy lifestyle and is currently in the market. The Startup has been recognized by EO GSEA Pune where it ranked amongst the top 8, similarly by IBS Magarpatta and others.

Farm-o-Fresh

They are solving a problem of adulteration of milk and milk products by delivering fresh and healthy milk directly from farmers to customer's doorstep within few hours of milking. They prefer ecofriendly packaging for their products rather than plastic pouches, as direct contact of food with plastic can be harmful.

Coverezy

Coverezy aims to be a product based startup by providing easy to use, time saving vehicle covers using a self retracting mechanism currently in it's prototyping phase. The idea has been appreciated and validated by GSEA ranking in Top 10 Startups in Pune. Coverezy has also been a finalist in Eureka (Business Plan competition by IIT Bombay) and aims to enter the market real soon.

invenzee

Invenzee Technologies is a research based Startup, which is targeted in solving problem statements in different domains with innovative approach and IPR. Invenzee has International and National recognition and 4 Patents in different domains.

Mushak

E-commerce website dedicated for Lord Ganesh’s idol which is operational in 5 cities (Aurangabad, Jalna, Amravati, Sangli and Akola). They supported 70+ artists and 750+ idols were delivered in the 5 cities.
