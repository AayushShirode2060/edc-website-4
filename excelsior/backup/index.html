EXPLORE EXCELSIOR

Book the dates 24th & 25th April

Excelsior is an entrepreneurial summit showcasing the journeys of entrepreneurs, creators, and innovators who knew when to use first-order thinking and when to think out of the box. It is an event where young enthusiasts interact with successful industrial stalwarts and learn from their experiences and get inspired to innovate.

Rightly said by Greek philosopher Heraclitus, change is the only constant. The global market varies daily and latest trends are explored. This year at Excelsior 2021, the gap between these trends and the attendees will be infused. Let's get adapted to these changes and grasp this opportunity to achieve and excel the state of art.

microSPEAKS

Book the date 25th April

The Second Day of Excelsior, also known as MicroSpeaks is structured for the overall development of the attendees. This day plays a vital role in inculcating the skills required for their upcoming journey of start-ups, establishing contacts and building a strong network of like-minded people.

This day will serve the purpose of a platform for people to have meaningful and purpose-driven conversations with distinguished guests. Our goal is to bring together bright minds to have conversations that are idea-focused, and on a wide range of subjects, to foster learning, inspiration and wonder – and provoke conversations that matter.
