    Since 1999, Pantone's Color of the Year has influenced product development and purchasing decisions in multiple industries, including fashion, home furnishings, and industrial design, as well as product packaging and graphic design.

    Every year, Pantone's Color of the Year is meticulously selected through a comprehensive process that involves a keen examination of global influences. The Pantone Color Institute, led by color experts, explores diverse sources such as the entertainment industry, emerging artists, fashion, design trends, popular travel destinations, and socio-economic conditions. The color selection is also informed by advancements in technology, new materials, and even the impact of social media platforms.

    For the year [2024], Pantone has chosen "Peach Fuzz", a soft peach shade with a compassionate and nurturing quality, enriching mind, body, and soul, according to Laurie Pressman, Vice President of the Pantone Color Institute.
