INTRODUCTION

'BizzHour' is a competition for young aspiring entrepreneurs working on their nascent start-ups. It is inspired by the popular TV series, Shark Tank. This competition plays a vital role in inculcating the skills required for their upcoming journey of start-ups,establishing contacts and building a strong network of like-minded people. The participating teams will be pitching for investment followed by an additional task to be performed which will be unique for each qualified team.

FAQs

Will I get the task after I register?

Tasks will be given after you qualify the investors pitch round.Refer the rulebook for proper structure.

Will accomodation will be provided?

Yes, accomdation will be provided.Contact us for further details.

Will there be provision for food?

Lunch and water will be provided. Own snacks are allowed.

What is the eligibility criteria for business to participate?

Start-ups at any stage can participate in the event.

What is Bizz Hour?

BizzHour is a competition inspired by popular web series 'Shark Tank' with addition of specific tasks for each team.

How many people can form a team?

You can form a team of maximum 3 members. If you have more than 3 members in your start-up then only 3 members can register as a team and attend the event.

What will be the tasks?

Tasks are some unique problems that will be different for each of the participating teams.It will depend upon your current business phase and the domain of your idea
